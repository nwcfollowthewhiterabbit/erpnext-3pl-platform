# Client Product Management

## Business Decision

Clients manage their product cards through the existing client portal, not through ERPNext Desk.

ERPNext `Item` remains the warehouse master record. The client-facing layer is `Three PL Client Product`, which exposes only safe product fields and synchronizes them into `Item`.

## Current Implemented Behavior

Client portal section:

`/client/products/list`

Client-editable fields:

- client SKU;
- product name;
- description;
- UOM;
- barcode;
- photo;
- status;
- notes.

System-managed fields:

- owner client;
- ERPNext item code;
- sync status;
- sync error;
- last sync timestamp.

When a client creates or updates a product card, `scripts/sync_client_products.py` synchronizes allowed fields into ERPNext `Item`.

## Identity Rule

The business identity is:

`Owner Client + Client SKU`

The global ERPNext `item_code` is generated by the system. Clients should not be responsible for global ERPNext item codes.

## Soft Delete

Clients do not physically delete product master records.

To remove a product from active use, the client changes product status to `Inactive`. The sync processor then sets the linked ERPNext `Item.disabled = 1`.

This preserves stock history, receiving records, movements, and reports.

## Audit Trail

Changes are recorded in `Three PL Client Product Change Log`.

Tracked events:

- Created;
- Updated;
- Deactivated;
- Synced;
- Sync Failed.

The log stores old values, new values, changed user, change time, linked client product, and linked ERPNext item.

## Current Limits

- Excel import/export is not implemented yet.
- Product approval workflow is not implemented yet.
- Product image is captured as a product card attachment/image field; final production image handling still needs review.
- Sync currently runs through the versioned processor, not through a custom app hook.
