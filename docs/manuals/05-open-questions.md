# Open Questions

Use this list during client validation.

## Scanner Devices

- Will the warehouse use dedicated TSD/scanner devices?
- Is browser plus USB/Bluetooth barcode scanner enough for the first stage?
- Is offline scanning required?

## Multi-Client Inventory

- Should client ownership be enforced at stock balance level?
- Is document-level client tracking enough for the first phase?
- Can different clients' stock share the same physical warehouse location?

## Traceability

- Are serial numbers required?
- Are batch numbers required?
- Are expiry dates required?
- Are lot-level recalls needed?

## Storage Logic

- Fixed locations per SKU?
- Dynamic putaway?
- Mixed SKU per location?
- Mixed client per location?

## Integrations

- Will client shipment notifications arrive by CSV, email, API, or portal?
- Which courier/shipping systems are expected later?
- Do clients need status callbacks or exports?
